About Us

Ceylon Blue Range Tours is a premier travel agency dedicated to showcasing the natural wonders, rich culture, and warm hospitality of Sri Lanka.

Your Journey, Our Passion

With over 10 years of experience, we take pride in offering personalized travel experiences that cater to your unique interests and preferences. Our team of knowledgeable guides are locals who understand the island's hidden gems and can provide authentic insights into Sri Lankan life.

We specialize in customized tour packages that combine adventure, relaxation, cultural immersion, and wildlife experiences, ensuring you get the most out of your Sri Lankan journey.

At Ceylon Blue Range Tours, we are committed to sustainable tourism practices that respect the environment and benefit local communities.

Popular Destinations

Explore the diverse landscapes of Sri Lanka, from pristine beaches to misty mountains, ancient cities to lush wildlife sanctuaries.

Kandy

Explore the cultural capital of Sri Lanka, home to the sacred Temple of the Tooth Relic and surrounded by beautiful hills.

Sigiriya

Climb the ancient rock fortress, a UNESCO World Heritage site featuring remarkable frescoes and landscaped gardens.

Ella

A picturesque mountain village with stunning views, hiking trails, and the famous Nine Arch Bridge.

Galle

Visit the historic Galle Fort, with its colonial architecture, charming streets, and beautiful ocean views.

Yala National Park

Experience thrilling wildlife safaris and spot leopards, elephants, and numerous bird species in their natural habitat.

Mirissa

Relax on pristine beaches and enjoy whale watching excursions in this idyllic coastal paradise.
