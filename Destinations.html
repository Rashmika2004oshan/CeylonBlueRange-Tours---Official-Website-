Sigiriya Rock Fortress

An ancient rock fortress and palace ruins, known as the Lion Rock, featuring incredible frescoes and landscaped gardens. A UNESCO World Heritage Site that showcases Sri Lanka's rich historical heritage.

Kandy

The cultural capital of Sri Lanka, home to the sacred Temple of the Tooth Relic. Experience traditional Kandyan dance, explore beautiful botanical gardens, and enjoy the serene mountain landscape.

Yala National Park

Sri Lanka's most famous wildlife sanctuary, home to a variety of animals including leopards, elephants, and numerous bird species. Experience an unforgettable safari adventure in this diverse ecosystem.

Galle Fort

A historic fortified city with Dutch colonial architecture, located on the southwestern coast. Explore charming streets, boutique shops, museums, and enjoy stunning ocean views.

Nuwara Eliya

Known as "Little England", this hill station offers a cool climate, beautiful tea plantations, and scenic landscapes. Explore colonial-era buildings and enjoy the picturesque mountain scenery.

Bentota

Bentota is a resort town on Sri Lanka's southwest coast. Its long Bentota Beach stretches north, where it becomes a sandy strip known as Paradise Island, parallel to Bentota Lagoon. Coral-rich dive sites include Canoe Rock.
